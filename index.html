Schoolmate is an organisation that specializes in Comparative Education. We move around the world studying different school systems then we bring back values that suit our education system in Uganda. Our aim is to improve our Education: especially at this time when everyone believes that our education system and quality needs special attention.

Tourism and Exposure

As educators we understand that to stimulate learning, social interaction, creativity and fun, a student has to be exposed to many experiences. Schoolmate organizes school tours and exposure activities. What makes our tours different from all other kinds of tours is that they are arranged in a way that fosters learning. Education Technology

Education Technology does not only mean to have a computer laboratory as many people think. Through our Education Technology program we help schools to understand the meaning of education technology as an array of tools that are helpful in advancing learning. Then we work together with the school to acquire these tools through cost effective and sustainable means. Boost your lessons with meaningful teaching aids. Come to Schoolmate, because we know how to do it and we love learning.
